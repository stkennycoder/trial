About Kemebos Schools

Kemebos Schools is the premier educational institution in Tanzania, founded by Hon. Kaizirege in 2007 in Kagera, Bukoba, just 6km from Bukoba Municipal. Our institution has grown to become a beacon of academic excellence, consistently producing top-performing students in national examinations.

Hon. Kaizirege

Founder of Kemebos Schools

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."

At Kemebos Schools, our dedicated staff and teachers are committed to delivering the NECTA syllabuses while supplementing with additional extracurricular courses. This holistic approach ensures our students excel not only academically but also develop socially and personally.

We take pride in our teaching capabilities and our unwavering commitment to ensuring that every student at Kemebos Schools passes their examinations with flying colors. Our track record speaks for itself, with consistent top rankings in national results year after year.

Our Educational Programs

Kemebos Schools offer a comprehensive range of competent education from primary to secondary education, providing students with a diverse array of subjects and engaging extracurricular activities that stimulate both mind and body.

Primary Education (Grade 1 - Grade 7)

At Kemebos Schools, we take pride in offering an exceptional primary education journey from Grade 1 through Grade 7, where every child is nurtured to thrive academically, socially, and personally. Our dedicated educators create a vibrant and supportive learning environment, blending innovative teaching methods with a well-rounded curriculum to spark curiosity, build confidence, and lay a strong foundation for lifelong success.

With a focus on holistic development, we ensure each student receives individualized attention, fostering not just academic excellence but also character growth and creativity. At Kemebos, we don't just teach—we inspire, empower, and prepare your child for a bright future, making learning an exciting and rewarding adventure every step of the way.

Secondary Education (Form 1 - Form 4)

At Kemebos Schools, we offer an outstanding secondary education from Form 1 to Form 4, designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence they need to excel in their academic and future careers. Our dynamic curriculum covers essential subjects, delivered by passionate teachers who inspire a love for learning.

With a perfect blend of rigorous academics, hands-on experiences, and personalized support, we ensure every student reaches their full potential. At Kemebos, we don't just educate—we shape future leaders, critical thinkers, and innovators, preparing them for success in national exams and beyond. Join us, and let's unlock your child's brightest future together!

A-Level Education (Form 5 - Form 6)

At Kemebos Schools, we offer an exceptional A-Level education (Form 5 to Form 6) designed to shape future leaders, innovators, and scholars through a rigorous and inspiring academic journey. Students can choose from specialized combinations, each tailored to unlock their intellectual potential and career aspirations.

With expert educators, cutting-edge resources, and a nurturing learning environment, we cultivate critical thinking, creativity, and excellence, ensuring students excel in both national and global examinations. At Kemebos, we don't just prepare students for exams; we empower them for university success and beyond, turning ambitions into achievements.

Available Combinations:

  • PCM – (Physics, Chemistry, Advanced Mathematics)
  • PCB – (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • PGM – (Physics, Geography, Advanced Mathematics)
  • HGL – (History, Geography, Language)
  • ECA – (Economics, Commerce, Accounting)
