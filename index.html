    About Us

    Kemebos Schools is one of the most prestigious educational institutions in Tanzania, established in 2007 with a vision to provide quality education that transforms lives. Our school has consistently ranked among the top performers in national examinations, including holding the first position in the national exams for CSEE in 2012.

    Located in the heart of Kagera Region, Bukoba Tanzania, just 6km from Bukoba Municipal, our campus provides a serene environment perfect for learning and personal growth. We focus on our students' enlightenment, care, and success both academically and socially.

    At Kemebos, we believe education is more than just academics. We nurture well-rounded individuals through our comprehensive programs that include leadership training, sports, arts, and community service. Our alumni have gone on to excel in various fields both nationally and internationally, a testament to the strong foundation they received at our institution.
