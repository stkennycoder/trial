Admissions

Advanced Level

Students joining Form Five should have the following qualifications:

  • To join our vibrant learning community, applicants must have sat for the most recent CSE Examination and achieved Division I.
  • For Science Streams (PCM/PCB): A minimum of 6 points in the combination subjects is required, with no grade lower than a B in any subject.
  • For Arts Streams (EGM, HE, HKL): We welcome passionate learners with Division II, provided they score at least a B in their combination subjects.
  • Prospective students can collect application forms directly from the school.

Admission Process:
Each candidate will undergo a tailored written and oral interview based on their grade level, ensuring we identify and nurture their unique potential.

At Kemebos, we don't just admit students—we invest in future leaders. Ready to begin your journey?

Secondary Level

Form One Admission Requirements at Kemebos Schools

We're looking for bright young scholars ready to excel! To join our Form One class, students must meet these criteria:

Educational Background

  • Must have successfully completed primary education
  • Must have passed the Standard Seven National Examinations

Entrance Evaluation

  • All applicants take our Science and Mathematics entrance exam
  • Must achieve a minimum score of 80% to advance

Two-Stage Selection Process

  • First Round: Multiple-choice written test (designed to assess true understanding beyond guessing)
  • Second Round: Personal oral interview for top performers

Final Admission Steps

  • Successful candidates receive official joining instructions
  • Enrollment is confirmed upon completion of first payment

At Kemebos, we don't just admit students - we welcome future leaders!

Primary Education

For primary education the student must have the following qualifications:

Admission Requirements for Grade One at Kemebos Schools

We welcome bright young learners to begin their primary education journey with us! To join our Grade One class, students must:

  • Be graduates from a recognized Nursery program
  • Have successfully mastered foundational skills in:
    • Writing (letter formation & basic words)
    • Reading (simple words and sentences)
    • Arithmetic (numbers 1-100 & basic operations)

Our admission process ensures every child has the necessary building blocks to thrive in our vibrant learning environment.

Early Years Admissions at Kemebos Schools

We welcome bright young learners to begin their educational journey in our nurturing kindergarten program:

Age Requirements:

  • Children may join our kindergarten program starting at 3 years of age

Program Structure:

Our kindergarten is divided into three developmental stages:

  • KG1 (Foundational year)
  • KG2 (Building year)
  • KG3 (Transition year)

Admission Process:

Each applicant undergoes a gentle, age-appropriate assessment:

  • Verbal evaluation to observe communication skills
  • Interactive activities to assess readiness for their joining level

At Kemebos, we make first school experiences joyful while ensuring every child is placed in the perfect environment to blossom!

How to Apply
